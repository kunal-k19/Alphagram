PORTRAIT

SHIVRAJ KINNUR

Hi! I'm Shivraj, an amateur photographer based out of Aurangabad, India. A Computer Science and Engineering graduate, who, instead of enjoying drafting lines of codes on a computer screen, has always found comfort and passion in framing memories with a click of the shutter. Over the course of several years, this passion has blossomed into somewhat of a dream. What is that you ask? The dream is simple, to teach myself the ins and outs, the dos and don'ts and the whats and hows of photography, and to grow not only as a photographer, but as a person who understands the simplicity, the beauty and the story behind every moment he captures.
